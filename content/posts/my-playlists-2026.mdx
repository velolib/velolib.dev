In this blog post, I'll go over some of my public playlists on Spotify. I'll talk about the inspiration behind them, the songs I've added, and the general vibe of each playlist.

## chronological anime playlist

This playlist contains all of the songs I've listened to and liked while watching anime. It's hard for me to pick one highlight from the playlist, but if I had to choose one it'd have to be kessoku band. I think if I hadn't listened to them I wouldn't have listened to anime-derived music as much today.

## Everything I Know About Laufey

<SpotifyEmbed uri="spotify:playlist:3X8ZcYKaSqxza2qVt17u6S" />

Laufey has been my #1 artist on Spotify for more than 2 years now. I discovered her music from when I was printing an assignment and my mom was playing music on shuffle on her Mac. When I heard "Beautiful Stranger" and was instantly hooked.

This playlist is just all of my liked Laufey songs, and the name is a play on one of her albums, "Everything I Know About Love". My favorite song from this playlist is clearly "Beautiful Stranger", because it's the song that got me into her music.

I will say my biggest regret this year is not getting tickets to see her live.s

## Kero Kero Bonito

<SpotifyEmbed uri="spotify:playlist:2BzA6C5YnLKS3CVnrv3Iv1" />

Unlike the previous playlist, this one isn't specially named (I ran out of ideas). Though, you may notice the playlist cover is not just the covers of the albums. Kero Kero Bonito is a band that I discovered from YouTube, and I've been listening to them ever since.

This playlist is a mix of all of my liked KKB songs, basically their best songs from all 3 of their albums. One thing I like about their discography is that all 3 of their albums are very different from each other, but they all still have that KKB style. My favorite song from this playlist is "The Princess and the Clock".

## (Unicode Piano)

<SpotifyEmbed uri="spotify:playlist:1PAgdJVRPDTXnntEWYMBYC" />

This playlist is a bit different from the previous ones. It's a playlist of mostly instrumental songs that have the piano as the main instrument. I named it "█▄█▄██▄█▄█▄█" because it's a bunch of Unicode characters that look like a piano.

You might think that this playlist only contains classical music, but it's actually mostly contemporary music. There are a lot of instrumental piano track I like, and they're not strictly contemporary or classical. I like to listen to this playlist when I'm studying or working, because it's very calming and helps me focus. My favorite song from this playlist is "Exhale" by Lena Raine.

## Games

<SpotifyEmbed uri="spotify:playlist:6B0VaGUeGBeEUtD2tW2pKT" />

Not the most interesting playlist, but it's a playlist of all of the songs from video games that I like. It's chronological, so the first songs are from the games I played when I was a kid, and the latest songs are from the games I played recently.

My favorite song from this playlist is "Farewell" by Lena Raine. It's from the game Celeste, which is one of my favorite games of all time.

## Read Only Memories

<SpotifyEmbed uri="spotify:playlist:3RgKI57NdKrKFuHQUOcz5w" />

This playlist is a collection of my 100 favorite mostly instrumental mostly electronic songs. I named it "Read Only Memories" from the name of Daft Punk's album, because I think it fits the vibe of the playlist.

This playlist features more well-known electronic music artists such as Camellia, Snail's House, meganeko, and more. Right now my favorite song from this playlist is "starlight speedway" by floopy, but sometimes when I listen to this playlist I like to reorder the songs. Did I mention this playlist is ordered by how much I like the songs?
